Fast Memory Cues

Cholinergic: wet, slow, moving. Think saliva, diarrhea, urination, small pupils, slow heart.
Anticholinergic: dry, fast, backed up. Think dry mouth, constipation, retention, big pupils, fast heart.
Question trick: ask yourself whether the patient looks more like “rest and digest” or more like “dried out and revved up.”
